Feature Overview

Manual Control

Prism Lights allows for changing the on/off state, brightness, and color of the lights. This is the simplest form of control and applies to individual lights as well as groups of lights.


Automated Control

Automated control allows for automatically adjusting bulbs according to sunset and sunrise times, cycling colors, timers, and creating schedules for when bulbs turn on and off throughout the week.


Favorites

Favorites allow for saving favorite bulb states for accessing them later.

Music Synchronization

Music synchronization allows for controlling the lights by associating each light to a particular frequency range, setting frequency ranges, beats per minute of the music, and maximum brightness that the lights can be.

Position Tracking

Using Bluetooth beacons, Prism Lights tracks your position inside your home and turns your lights on and off as you move from room to room.



Voice Commands

Allows for controlling the lights using English. For example, "Turn kitchen lights purple."
